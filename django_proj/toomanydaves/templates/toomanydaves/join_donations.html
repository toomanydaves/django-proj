Too Many Daves is free and open to anyone who wishes to join and always will be!

Too Many Daves is and intends to remain an open book. It has no investors or other hidden entities clamouring for control nor any significant revenue streams it depends on. It is simply the happy result of the goodwill and cumulative work of like-minded individuals sharing their time, energy and expertise in the name of progress and in support of the creative process.

In short, like the software it's built on[1], Too Many Daves exists proudly in the tradition of Open Source.

However, though we make it a point to use, contribute to[2], and credit[3] open and free tools wherever possible, Too Many Daves still has some unavoidable operating costs[4] and will continue to pursue the occasional bricks-and-mortar initiative deemed worth spending money on[5]. So if you happen to be in the position to, {% if not user.is_authenticated %} in addition to signing up, {% endif %} you'd absolutely be helping us be able to better fulfill our mission by contributing to our war chest.

Regardless of amount even if it's just for a cup of coffee donations are always much appreciated, will be recognized[6] and put to good use!
