Too Many Daves is free and open to everyoneand always will be

Too Many Daves is, and intends to remain, an open book.
Like the software it's built on, Too Many Daves is proud to exist in the tradition of Open Source. It has no investors to be beholden to nor any revenue streams it depends on. It is simply the happy result of a vision, elbow grease, some goodwill, and the ongoing and cumulative efforts of like-minded individuals sharing their time, energy, passion and expertise in pursuit of progress and support of the creative process.

However Too Many Daves still needs money to function

Though the point is to use, contribute to and credit open and free tools wherever possible, Too Many Daves still has some unavoidable operating costs. It would also like to pursue the occasional brick-and-mortar initiative, it deems worth spending money on.
