Too Many Daves is free and open to anyone who wishes to join and always will be!

At this point, Too Many Daves is an open book. It has no investors or other hidden entities clamouring for control nor any significant revenue streams it depends on. It is simply the happy result of good will and the dogged work of like-minded individuals sharing their time, energy and expertise in the name of progress.

Like the software it's built on[1], it exists proudly in the tradition of open-source.

Though we make it a point to use (and credit[2]) open and free tools wherever possible, Too Many Daves still has some unavoidable operating costs[3] and will pursue the occasional initiative deemed worth spending money on[4].

So if you happen to be in the position to, in addition to signing up, you can certainly help us better fulfill our mission by contributing to our war chest.

Regardless of amount even if it's just for a cup of coffee donations are always greatly appreciated, and will be recognized[5] and put to good use!
