You want to contribute to an individual project?

Terrific! Projects are always looking for help.

Most projects are actively searching for volunteers, and some, for new talent to join their teams. Typically most projects provide information about what they need and how to be in touch directly on their website. This should definitely be your first recourse. However{% if not user.is_authenticated %} if you're a member{% endif %}, you may also want to send a message to other members affliated with the project{% if not user.is_authenticated %} (if you're not, it's probably a good time to join){% endif %}. When you're ready, go to the Portfolio, locate the project, and you should be able to find the information you need.
