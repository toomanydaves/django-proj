Too Many Daves is free and openand always will be

In the proud tradition of Unix and Wikipedia, and like the software it's built on, Too Many Daves adheres to the tenets of Open Source. It has no investors to be beholden to nor any revenue streams it depends on. It is simply the happy result of a vision plus the ongoing and cumulative efforts of like-minded individuals contributing their time, energy, passion and expertise in pursuit of progress and support of the creative process.

This notwithstanding, it still needs some money to function

Though the point is to use, contribute to and credit open and free tools wherever possible, and in general, to keep expenses to a minimum, Too Many Daves still has some unavoidable operating costs, as well as the want to sponsor the occasional brick-and-mortar initiative.
