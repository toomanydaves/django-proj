You want to contribute to an individual project?

Fantastic! Projects are always looking for help.

Most projects are actively searching for volunteers and some for new talent to join their teams. Typically they will provide information about what they need and how to contact them directly on their website. This should be your first recourse.

However if you're a member of Too Many Daves, you may also want to contact other members affliated with the project directly through the site. (If you're not, it's probably a good time to join!)

When you're ready, go to the project in the portfolio and you should be able to find the information you need.
