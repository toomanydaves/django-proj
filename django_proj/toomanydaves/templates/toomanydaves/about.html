Did I ever tell you that Mrs. McCave
Had twenty-three sons and she named them all Dave?
Well she did.  And that wasn't a smart thing to do...

Thankfully life presents us with immeasurable opportunities to exercise our creativity.
Sadly though as was the case in the naming of the Daves, if we don't act, they simply pass us by.

The moral of that poem inspires the mission of this network. To be vigilant, and fight on as many fronts possible to support exhibitions of creativity and personal investment wherever they are called for.

This is a platform showcasing the Light of Endeavor through a diverse portfolio of exemplary initiatives from committed individuals with the shared mission to foster engagement, agency and creativity within a particular context.

To spread love. It's the Brooklyn way!

All are welcome!!!
Check out the projects! Check out the members! Join the fight!
