What is Too Many Daves? Backstory

Too Many Daves is foremost a platform for curating, managing and showcasing a diverse portfolio of exemplary projects. Their shared purpose, to foster engagement, agency and creativity.

To spread love. It's the Brooklyn way!

A Portfolio of Projects.

Too Many Daves takes its name from the eponymous poem by Theodor Geisel, aka Dr. Seuss.

"Did I ever tell you that Mrs. McCave
Had twenty-three sons and she named them all Dave?
Well she did.  And that wasn't a smart thing to do..."

Read the entire text

On its surface, Too Many Daves is a poem about names and naming things. Its playful spirit captures the character of this network. But make no mistake, it is also a powerful cautionary tale; one whose moral inspires the mission of this organization.

A Name.

To be vigilant and active

On as many fronts as possible,
To promote exhibitions of creativity and personal investment,
Wherever they might benefit from a little encouragement.
