The Platform and its Portfolio

Too Many Daves is foremost a platform for showcasing a diverse portfolio of exemplary projects. Their shared purpose, to foster engagement, agency and creativity.

To spread love. It's the Brooklyn way!

The Naming of Too Many Daves

Thankfully life presents us with immeasurable opportunities to exercise our own creativity. Sadly though as was the case in the naming of the Daves, if we don't act, they will simply pass us by.

On a Mission from God

The moral of its namesake poem, Too Many Daves, inspires the mission of this Network:

To be vigilant and active

On as many fronts as possible,
To promote exhibitions of creativity and personal investment,
Wherever they might benefit from a little encouragement.
