Cologne, Germany
Fall 2017

Too Many Daves has its origins almost twenty years ago and nearly half a world away at Habibi's Hutch, a wonderful preschool in Austin, Texas, where I had the good fortune to first encounter the little-known poem.

It really didn't take more than a couple times reading it aloud and the sound of the little ones' laughter to recognize something special in its text. As I've followed along my path, it's stayed with me, providing inspiration along the way.

That way, which began teaching children, eventually lead out of the classroom and to the following projects:

School in the Cloud

The thoughtful and creative integration of 21st century technology has boundless potential to democratize and enliven educational opportunities. I was already living in Europe when I set myself to the task of learning to code for real. I wanted to focus on writing educational software.

What resulted is School in the Cloud.

You Better

The birth of my two sons has taught me a lot about the person I want to be and what I want to accomplish. As my thoughts have turned more existential, I've used my programming skills on an app to help me, and whoever else, set and reach personal goals.

To let it shine[4]!

This is You Better.

Lebenskunst

Back in Austin, and previously and afterwards in New York, as well as a teacher, I'd been a student myself. I learned how to draft, weld, solder glass and work with wood I began making things in earnest.

In the intervening years, I've met many fellow artists, makers and designers working in a variety of contexts. And over time, I realized something that I needed to create for myself and these like-minded folks a platform where works of art can be curated and past on to others in a way both sustainable for the artists, designers and makers, as well as affordable for everyday folks looking for authentic goods.

This is Lebenskunst.

One life's work

These experiences define who I am, form the core projects in the current portfolio and reflect the spirit of Too Many Daves.

And they are in great company!

They are surrounded by many other similarly intentioned, innovative projects. Some in collaboration. Some just known to Too Many Daves as worthy of admiration. All in various stages of realization. All, I'd submit, worthy of your attention.

Program or Be Programmed[5]

Finally, as a social network Too Many Daves is in its infancy.

In fact it's literally just crossed that most crucial threshold of existence it's come into the world or as the case may be, the World Wide Web. What was even recently just a construct, a vision in my mind, finally has a form to exist on its own, and a way to make itself known.

Heck, it has you reading about it!

And should you be as interested in what you see as I hope you will be, there are plenty of opportunities to contribute a verse[2]. Even though it's still very much my baby, Too Many Daves was always meant to exist apart from me, with plenty of room to accomodate anyone who wants to stand for self-expression, creativity, tolerance and wonder.

Thanks very much for hearing me out.
