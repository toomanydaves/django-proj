This is me, Sean Jacke, the prime mover of Too Many Daves, the one who started things in motion.

In what follows, I'm gonna do my best to bring you up to speed.

Lay of the Land

As a platform, Too Many Daves is in its infancy. It's just crossed that most crucial threshold of existence – it's come into the world, or as the case may be, the World Wide Web.

What was once just a construct, or vision in my mind, now has a form to exist on its own and a way to make itself known. It has you, reading about it!

Beginnings

Too Many Daves has its origins twenty years ago and a half a world away, on a beach in Northern California when I decided to become a teacher. It was several years later, working at a preschool in Austin, Texas, when I encountered Too Many Daves, the poem, for the first time.

School in the Cloud

Always up for adventure and wanting to change the world, it was living in Europe when I learned how to program in order to develop educational software .

Lebenskunst

Back in Austin, and previously and afterwards in New York, I'd been not just a teacher, but a student as well. In my spare time, I learned how to draft, weld, solder glass and work with wood – I began making things in earnest.

In the time since, I've met many artists, makers and designers. And I realized that I had something to offer us all. So I began work on a platform.

You Better

After the birth of my two sons, I started learning even more about the person I want to be. I decided to use my programming skills to produce an app designed to help me, and whoever else, set and reach personal goals.

Program or Be Programmed

These experiences and projects define who I am and form the current core of Too Many Daves:

  • School in the Cloud
  • Lebenskunst
  • You Better

However they are not alone in the portfolio of Too Many Daves. They are surrounded by many other projects, worthy of equal attention. Some from collaborators. Some just known to Too Many Daves as a object of admiration. All in various stages of realization. All, I'd submit, worthy of your attention.

By joining Too Many Daves you will be showing your support, for these projects and putting yourself in the position to work on them directly.

So if you're interested in what you see, there are lots of ways to get involved! We'd be honored if you would!
