Program or Be Programmed

In addition to a portfolio of projects, Too Many Daves is intended to be a network and forum, a project itself, devoted to creativity. In this respect, it is still in its infancy; in fact it's literally just crossed that most crucial threshold of existence it's come into the world or as the case may be, the World Wide Web. I can't predict where it will go from here, but hey, it has you reading about it, and really, how exciting!

And should you be enamoured with what you see, please don't hesitate to join in, to contribute a verse. Even though it's still very much my baby, Too Many Daves was always meant to exist apart from me, with room to accomodate anyone who wants to stand with me for self-expression, creativity, tolerance and wonder.

So thanks very much for hearing me out!

If you haven't already, I do sincerely hope you'll join me in this endeavor.
