Too Many Daves! has its origins twenty years ago and nearly half a world away at Habibi's Hutch, a wonderful preschool in Austin, TX, where I had the good fortune to first encounter the little-known poem.

It didn't take more than a couple times reading it aloud and the immediate sound of little ones' laughter to notice something special in its text. As I've followed along my path, its message has stayed with me, providing inspiration along the way.
