Too Many Daves! has its origins twenty years ago and nearly half a world away at Habibi's Hutch, a wonderful preschool in Austin, TX, where I had the good fortune to first encounter the little-known poem.

It took a couple times reading it aloud and the immediate sound of little ones' laughter until I started to appreciate what was so special in its text. As I've followed along my path, its message has stayed with me, providing real inspiration along the way.
